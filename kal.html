Kaleah's Games

These are the games I have created throughout the year.



TAG!

This is the infamous game of tag!(but with a twist;D) You are the bear and you are trying to eat the taco/tag the taco to earn points. BUT BE WARNED! The bat will try to tag you and take ALL of your points and send you back to zero!



Baseball

In this game, you need to hit the baseball in order to get points.



Paddle Game

Don't let the ball touch the red line! Use your mouse to move the paddle.



Maze Game

In this game, you need to use the arrows to move the cat to the cake without hitting ANY of the lines. This will test your accuracy in a fun way! :D
